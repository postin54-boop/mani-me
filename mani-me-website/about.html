About Mani Me

Connecting families across continents with reliable parcel delivery from the UK to Ghana.

Our Story

Mani Me was founded with a simple mission: to make sending parcels from the UK to Ghana as easy as possible. We understand the importance of staying connected with family and loved ones, no matter the distance.

Having experienced the challenges of international shipping firsthand, our founders set out to create a service that is reliable, affordable, and transparent. Today, we've helped thousands of customers send parcels, gifts, and essential items to their loved ones in Ghana.

From our humble beginnings, we've grown into a trusted name in UK-Ghana logistics, but our commitment remains the same: to deliver your parcels safely and on time, every time.
